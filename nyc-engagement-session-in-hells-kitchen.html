New York City Engagement Session in Hell's Kitchen

Your engagement session is the best way to shake the anxiety and lil awkward moments that inevitably occur and truly be present in each others presence and watch as the rest will take care of itself. The type of clothes you wear or location we choose are secondary to the connection you have with your partner. Julissa and Mitchell were very fun to be around and were truly down to do whatever and just wanted to have a great time in their session.

Nothing was off limits even Mitchell picking up Juli and putting her on his shoulders half way through the session, these two knew how to be themselves and make the session about one another.

Pizza, fun couple, and Hudson Yards/Hell's Kitchen area as your back drop, is just a recipe for good moments and fun times.

Your NYC engagement session in is your time to shine and get comfortable and not just being in front of the camera but by also forming a partnership and trust with your photographer.
