Your Wedding Photography Experience

Services

The Approach

Being present in every moment, and capturing the day as it unfolds naturally with a relaxed documentary style approach creating memories you'll cherish for a lifetime. I'll be there to guide you when needed and give you space to enjoy each moment as it comes. There to capture everything in a genuine and authentic way and all the in between moments.

Personalized Experience

PERMISSION TO BE YOU

Connection is everything. This all starts by getting to know you and what matters to you the most. Our connection will guide my approach at how I will capture your story. With your trust in me and the process, we can build the perfect environment so you can experience the day in an organic and authentic way.

Empathy through the lens

LIVE FOR THE MOMENT

I've been in your shoes before and having done this myself, I know what it feels like to be in front of the camera. My experience has shaped my perspective on how to perfectly capture your day. Being fully present at my own wedding was the most important reason why I am able to have photos that I can look back on and say "yeah that's me and that's my personality". This is the very reason why I'm a photographer and the experience I strive to give you.

what does it look like to work with me...

1. Discovery Call

After your initial inquiry, we would hop on a video call or in person meetup to chat and get a vibe for one another, see if its a match. I'll see what truly matters to you, and piece together what you have envisioned for your dream day.

2. Strategy Planning

This is the nitty gritty, where I work together to help you navigate your day and sculpt the timeline of events and photos that are your must haves for your wedding. Then we can dive deep into curating something special, unique and get that excitement going for your dream day.

3. Wedding Day

Woohoo! The fun begins and magic happens. Your day will be documented in its purest form on top of all the in between moments and captruing the little details that mean the most in telling the story of your entire day. For a wedding day, I end up delivering on average 60 - 100 photos per hour in a day.

4. Deliverables

Its time to sit back and bask on your photos, I always suggest you grab your favorite drink of choice and go through your photos together and reminisce on how special you felt that day, make it a date night while your at it. Your high resolution images will be delivered in 4 - 6 weeks. Your images will be hosted on a online private gallery with a password protected and shareable link and get to view your day from so many perspectives.
