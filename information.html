Frequently Asked Q's

HOW WOULD YOU DESCRIBE YOUR EDITING STYLE?

I've been on the journey to what images speak to me and what i enjoy in my photgraphy career. When I found film and the colors that film produces I immediately fell in love and knew I had to incorporate it into my own work. My style is a true to life filmic color palette. So you can look back in 20years and still see yourself and how you actually looked as you were on that day with a nostalgic hint to your photos.

HOW MANY PHOTOS DO YOU DELIVER?

For a wedding day, I shoot alot and often and I end up choosing and delivering on average 60 - 100 photos per hour in a day give or take, could be less/could be more depending on the hours at the event coverage.

WHEN WILL WE RECEIVE OUR PHOTO GALLERY?

Your High-Res images will be delivered in 2 - 4 weeks. Your images will be hosted on a online private gallery with a password protected and shareable link.

DO I NEED A SECOND PHOTOGRAPHERS FOR MY WEDDING?

It all depends on the kind of coverage you desire for your day. We recommend having two photographers for weddings with more than 120 guests, or when couples are getting ready in distant locations.

Do you do destination weddings?

Absolutely! When? and where? Lets go!! I love to work in different destinations and I'm ready and willing anywhere to capture your moments. Destination packages are bespoke to you and what you really want shot. Whether you want your whole week including welcome party session, beachday, etc., or just for the Wedding its really custom made package for you.
