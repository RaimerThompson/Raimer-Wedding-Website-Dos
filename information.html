Frequently Asked Q's

HOW MANY PHOTOS DO YOU DELIVER?

For a wedding day, I end up delivering on average 60 - 100 photos per hour in a day give or take, could be less/could be more depending on the hours at the event coverage.

WHEN WILL WE RECEIVE OUR PHOTO GALLERY?

Your High Res images will be delivered in 4 - 6 weeks. Your images will be hosted on a online private gallery with a password protected and shareable link.

DO I NEED A SECOND PHOTOGRAPHER FOR MY WEDDING?

It all depends on the kind of coverage you desire for your day. We recommend having two photographers for weddings with more than 80 - 120 guests, or when couples are getting ready in distant locations.

Do you do destination weddings?

Absolutely! When? and where? Lets go!! I love to work in different destinations and I'm ready and willing anywhere to capture your moments. Destination packages are bespoke to you and what you really want shot. Whether you want your whole week including welcome party session, beachday, etc., or just for the Wedding its really custom made package for you.
