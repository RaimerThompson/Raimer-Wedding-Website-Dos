Welcome

I'm Raimer, but you can call me Ray

My wedding was the start of my photography journey. It was pivotal in me finding my true self, I literally said to myself that morning "Im going to live this day to my fullest, without a care in the world of how I looked, acted, or sounded" and it was truly the greatest day of my life. Marrying my wife Grace was the best and smartest decision I've ever made (she's my bestfriend and tag team partner).

I live my life every day giving as many positive vibes as I can to the world, and thats the attitude I bring to wedding day, engagement sessions, etc., I'm all about creating a bespoke experience, curated just for you and bonding with my couples. Its truly my hope and mission that on your special day you too can feel that you can live your day to the fullest.

What Makes Me Tick!

Keep it relaxed, playful, and real

Putting smiles on people's faces brings me so much joy and validation for why I do what I do. Love seeing the change that happens when you can finally drop your shoulders and become present especially with your loved ones right by your side.

Whether at the DJ Booth, or Karoake nights, I'm always humming to a tune, and you best believe I'll be joining you and your loved ones on the dance floor and grooving my way in for all the epic shots.
